# Product Vision

PHONARA is a Korean-first, globally extensible rewards, wallet, game, and trading platform centered on PHON.

## Positioning

- Korean users: easy app-tech and side-income UX.
- Power users: transparent PHON/USDT wallet, games, and trading.
- Operators: 1-person automated Admin with exception queues.
- Global future: i18n, country policies, PWA, and reliable support.
